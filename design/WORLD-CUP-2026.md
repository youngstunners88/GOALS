# FIFA World Cup 2026 — $GOALS Context

> The biggest stage. The highest stakes. Every kick is a trade.

## Why World Cup 2026

FIFA World Cup 2026 is hosted across **USA, Canada, and Mexico**. It is the largest global betting and attention event in sports. For $GOALS, this is our **Layer 3 launch window**.

We do not simulate full 90-minute matches. That is Layer 1 (commoditized). We focus on **three high-tension micro-moments** where emotion, probability, and capital intersect:

1. **Penalty Shootout** — Pure 1v1 psychology + high variance
2. **Corner Kicks** — Collective teamplay + set-piece chaos
3. **Free Kicks Outside the Box** — Precision risk vs. reward

Each moment is a **game mechanic** and a **DeFi primitive**.

## Narrative Frame

> *"You don't own the player. You own the moment."*

Users hold **Moment NFTs** tied to specific players in specific matches. The NFT's value updates based on what happens in those 3 moments. Agents trade on behalf of holders using real-time data + prediction market arbitrage.

## Moat

Generic sportsbooks offer odds. $GOALS offers:
- **Ownership** of the moment (NFT)
- **Programmable strategy** (agent-driven staking/lending/arbitrage)
- **Learning** from execution data (which moments, which players, which markets)

This is **Layer 3**: the system learns from every World Cup match and improves its models for the next one.
